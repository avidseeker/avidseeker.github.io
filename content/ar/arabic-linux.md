## Projects
## Arabic locales project
There are [23 Arabic countries with over 400 million Arabic
speakers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_countries_where_Arabic_is_an_official_language#Sovereign_states_where_Arabic_is_an_official_language).
And yet, there are only [a couple](https://i.imgur.com/MAJsi6r.png) of Arabic
locales and variants available.

This list contains the ways to contribute:

1. [gnome](https://l10n.gnome.org/teams/)
2. [Fedora](https://translate.fedoraproject.org/)
3. [KDE](https://community.kde.org/Get_Involved/translation)
4. [Ubuntu](https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu-translations/+question/703014)
