## Simple graphics
The concept of Markdown is really great. Simple syntax. Focus on what you write.
The goal is to have a high-level graphics language with this concept: syntax is
readable and elegant. All you have to focus on is writing. Think of a mindmap
or a flowchart.

* ASCII Mindtree from commandline: [tree --fromtabfile](https://gitlab.com/OldManProgrammer/unix-tree/-/issues/21)
* Mermaidjs
* Tikz
* asymptote
* Inkscape
